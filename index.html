Who We Are

About Power Fitness Gym

Power Fitness Gym is a modern training facility in the heart of Riyadh. We combine cutting-edge equipment, professional coaching, and a motivating atmosphere to help you achieve the best version of yourself. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, we have the tools and expertise to fuel your transformation.

Modern Equipment

State-of-the-art machines and free weights from leading global brands. Every piece of equipment is meticulously maintained for your safety and performance.

Certified Trainers

Our internationally certified coaches bring years of experience and a passion for fitness. Every trainer is dedicated to your personal growth and results.

Result Focused Training

We don't just train — we transform. Every program is designed with measurable goals in mind, ensuring you see real progress in strength, endurance, and body composition.
